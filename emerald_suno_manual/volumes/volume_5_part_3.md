126. **[The 15-Second TikTok Hook - "Main Character" Energy]**
    - **Style:** `[Euphoric Pop] [Neon Synths] [High-Energy Female Vocal] [Polished]`
    - **Lyrics:**
      ```
      [Hook]
      I'm the main character in my own movie,
      Sparkles in my eyes, everything is groovy!
      [Energy: Explosive]
      Watch me shine, watch me move,
      I've got nothing to lose!
      ```

    - **Style:** `[Swoosh Sound] [Orchestral Hit] [Low Drones] [Tense]`

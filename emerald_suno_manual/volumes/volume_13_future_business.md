TV, Film, and YouTube ads need "Functional Music" that doesn't distract from the message.
- **The "Background" Blueprint:** Use `[Corporate] [Upbeat] [Polished] [Low Dynamic Range] [Instrumental]`.
- **The "Emotional" Blueprint:** Use `[Cinematic] [Minimalist Piano] [Pensive] [Slow]`.

#### 2.2 The Ethics of Cloning
Suno v5.5 allows "Voices" (Cloning).
1. **Always** get permission if you are cloning a living human voice.
2. Use Cloning for "Identity Consistency" (your own vocal brand) rather than "Identity Theft" (imitating famous artists).
3. Professional platforms (Spotify/Apple) are increasingly using AI detectors. If your vocal sounds too much like a famous artist, your track will be removed.
